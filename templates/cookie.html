‚ùì FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Are my cookies secure?

Yes! Your cookies are stored in your personal MULTIPASS, encrypted and accessible only by you and your AI. No one else can access them.

How long do cookies stay valid?

Generally between 2 weeks and 6 months depending on the website. If your cookies expire, just re-export and re-upload a new file.

Can I upload cookies from multiple sites?

Yes! You can export and upload cookies from each site separately. The system will automatically detect the domain and store them correctly.

Will my normal browsing be affected?

No! Exporting in a private window ensures your normal session isn't disrupted. You can continue using sites normally.

What does my AI do with my cookies?

Your AI uses your cookies to:

  • YouTube: Sync your likes, download your favorite videos
  • Leboncoin: Search with your personal filters
  • Other sites: Access content as if it were you
